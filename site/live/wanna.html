Think you got what it takes to be beneficial to the She-Bytes team? Are you an "outside the box" kind of person?

Here are a few words of wisdom that struck a cord with all of us -- if you see yourself here, feel free to drop us an email with all the important info we need!!

Include your resume, as well as an idea of your skills, talents, goals, etc! We'll be in touch!

Here's to the crazy ones.
The misfits. The rebels.
The troublemakers.
The round pegs in the square holes.

The ones who see things differently.

They're not fond of rules.
And they have no respect for the status quo.

You can praise them, disagree with them, quote them, disbelieve them, glorify or vilify them.
About the only thing you can't do is ignore them.

Because they change things.
They invent. They imagine. They heal.

They explore. They create. They inspire.
They push the human race forward.

Maybe they have to be crazy.
How else can you stare at an empty canvas and see a work of art?
Or sit in silence and hear a song that's never been written?
Or gaze at a red planet and see a laboratory on wheels?

We make tools for these kinds of people.
While some see them as the crazy ones,
we see genius.

Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world,
are the ones who do.
